Common Questions

How much does coaching cost?
Standard coaching starts at $200/month. Premium tiers with more touchpoints and additional services are available. We'll discuss what makes sense for you on the strategy call.
Is there a minimum commitment?
Yes — 3 months minimum. Real results take consistent work, and this ensures we both have enough time to make meaningful progress. Most clients stay much longer.
How many clients do you take on?
I cap my roster to maintain quality. When spots are full, new applicants go on a waitlist. Applying now secures your place in line.
Do I need to be a competitive powerlifter?
No. I coach anyone who wants to get seriously strong — whether you're prepping for your first meet or just want to bench 315. The programming is built around your goals.
What platform do you use for coaching?
Programming is delivered through TrueCoach (or a custom spreadsheet if you prefer). Communication happens via WhatsApp or Voxer for daily check-ins, and Zoom for monthly calls.
What makes you different from other coaches?
I'm a USPA Junior National Champion and world record holder who actively competes. I'm not a random online coach with a certification — I've done what I'm coaching you to do, at the highest level.
